# Product Requirements Document: CrossFit Cidra Website

## 1. Executive Summary

CrossFit Cidra requires a modern, mobile-friendly website to establish their digital presence and attract potential members within a 30-minute radius of their location. The website will serve as the primary information hub and lead generation tool for the CrossFit box, presented entirely in Spanish.

The platform will focus on educating visitors about CrossFit's benefits, accessibility, and the unique value proposition of CrossFit Cidra. By implementing direct communication channels through WhatsApp, social media, and other messaging platforms, the website will facilitate immediate connection between prospective members and the box's staff.

The solution will emphasize visual content, testimonials, and clear information architecture to convert visitors into leads, ultimately supporting CrossFit Cidra's business growth and community development.

## 2. Product Overview

### Problem Statement
- Local residents lack easily accessible information about CrossFit Cidra and its offerings
- Potential members need education about CrossFit's accessibility and benefits
- The box needs a centralized platform to showcase their community and generate leads

### Solution Overview
A responsive, Spanish-language website that:
- Educates visitors about CrossFit
- Showcases the box's facilities and community
- Provides multiple communication channels
- Presents membership plans and pricing
- Displays social proof through member testimonials

### Target Users
Primary:
- Adults within 30-minute driving distance
- Fitness beginners to advanced athletes
- Spanish-speaking residents
- People seeking physical and mental health improvements

### Key Value Propositions
- Clear, accessible information about CrossFit
- Multiple easy contact options
- Visual proof of member success stories
- Mobile-first design for convenience
- Spanish-language content for local audience

## 3. Core Features & Requirements

### MVP Features
1. Homepage with key information and call-to-actions

4. Contact integration (WhatsApp, social media)
